Responsive Text Alignment

These classes allow you to change text alignment at different breakpoints:

  • .text-[sm/md/lg/xl]-left
  • .text-[sm/md/lg/xl]-center
  • .text-[sm/md/lg/xl]-right
  • .text-[sm/md/lg/xl]-justify

This text is centered on mobile, left-aligned on medium screens, and right-aligned on large screens.

<p class="text-center text-md-left text-lg-right">Responsive alignment text.</p>
